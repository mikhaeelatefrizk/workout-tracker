  • Double progression: add reps each session toward the top of the rep range, then when you hit the top on every set, add the smallest load and reset to the bottom. The Coach does this for you.
  • Effort: leave 1–3 reps in reserve on compounds; isolation can go to 0–2 RIR (near/at failure). Effort is what makes volume count.
  • Volume drives growth: aim 10–20 hard sets per muscle per week (see 📊 Stats). More isn't always better past ~20.
  • Rest ~2–3 min on compounds, 60–120 s on isolation — longer rest = more quality reps = more growth.
  • Load is forgiving (~6–20 reps all build muscle near failure). Form > weight; emphasise the full/stretched range. Log every set — the Coach reads your whole history.
